    We Are NexGenist

    Started in 2024 NexGenist grows exponentially through its research in technology. NexGenist.com, an initiative by our collegues towards development of research based software, embedded/electronics and mechanical systems for research & development purposes. With rapid growth in technology and innovation, NexGenist has now the largest collection of software, mechanical as well as electronics based self learning kits and more systems being researched every month. Due to this we now serve these kits in more than 50 colleges. NexGenist develops these self learning kits for students to help in technological research & developement for their final year projects along with IEEE papers. We build these systems to be used as a reverence for further development and studies by engineering students.
